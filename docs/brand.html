Colour carries meaning

calcy already colour-codes results by kind. That mapping is part of the brand — keep it consistent everywhere a result appears.

rate = 12k req/s→12 thousand req/s
5 km + 3 mi→9.83 km

Type

System sans for everything human (no font downloads, instant load), monospace for everything the engine touches — expressions and results. The split reinforces “friendly surface, precise core”.

displayabout 2.9 days
headingusually between 2 and 4
bodyPlain-spoken on the surface, rigorous underneath.
monochance(total < 20 day) → 0.31

Voice

Friendly precision: say the likely answer like a person would, then let the exact numbers be one tap away. Never pretend a single number is the whole truth.
